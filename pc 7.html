"The Australian Capital Territory houses Canberra, Australia’s capital, built between Sydney and Melbourne in the early 20th century. The federal district’s forest, farmland and nature reserves earn Canberra its nickname, the "Bush Capital.” The city's focal point is Lake Burley Griffin, filled with sailboats and kayaks .On opposite shores are the grand Australian War Memorial and the massive, strikingly modern Parliament House Area: 2,358 km² Capital: Canberra Chief minister: Andrew Barr Population: 4.31 lakhs "
