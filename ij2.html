Victoria is a state in southeast Australia. It encompasses mountains, national parks, wineries and surfing beaches' Melbourne, the state capital, has a warren of 19th-century ‘laneways’, and a central arts precinct. The Great Ocean Road follows the coast west to Port Campbell National Park, where the Twelve Apostles are limestone stacks on the edge of the ocean. Area: 227,444 km² Capital: Melbourne Population: 66.8 lakhs Premier: Daniel Andrews
