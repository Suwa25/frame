asmania, abbreviated as TAS, is an island state of Australia. It is located 240 km to the south of the Australian mainland, separated from it by Bass Strait. The state encompasses the main island of Tasmania, the 26th-largest island in the world, and the surrounding 1000 islands. Area: 68,401 km² Capital: Hobart Population: 5.41 lakhs (Sept 2020) Premier: Peter Gutwein
